Why Off-Plan

The Market Reality Right Now

Dubai and the wider UAE market offer a structured, regulated path to property ownership. Here's what serious buyers know before they move.

01

Entry Price Advantage

Off-plan prices are locked at launch, before construction appreciation. Early buyers consistently pay less per sqft than those waiting for ready units.

02

Flexible Payment Plans

60/40 and 70/30 structures mean you don't need the full amount upfront. Pay during construction, settle the balance on handover.

03

Zero Buyer's Fee

My advisory is paid by the developer. You get project access, due diligence and negotiation support at no direct cost to you.

04

High Rental Yield

Dubai consistently delivers 6–9% gross rental yields on off-plan units in growth corridors — well above global gateway city averages.
